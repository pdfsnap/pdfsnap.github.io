👤 Who Built This

👨‍💻
Mohammad Armaan
Founder & Developer · PDFSnap

I'm a software developer with 8 years of experience building web applications. I started PDFSnap in 2025 after watching friends pay for Adobe Acrobat just to do simple things — compress a file, rotate a page, merge two PDFs. These are operations that modern browsers can handle entirely on-device, with no server needed and no subscription required.

I built PDFSnap using PDF.js, pdf-lib, and the Canvas API. Every tool processes your files locally in your browser. Your documents never leave your device — that's something I take seriously, because I wouldn't want my own files sent to unknown servers either.

📖 Our Story

2024
The idea started when a friend needed to compress a PDF to email it for a job application. Every free tool online either had a file size limit, added a watermark, uploaded files to unknown servers, or pressured them to pay. There had to be a better way.
2025
PDFSnap launched with 8 core PDF tools — all running locally in the browser with zero server uploads. Core principle: powerful tools that respect user privacy, with no paywalls, no watermarks, and no upsells.
2026
Expanded to 16+ tools covering image editing, PDF conversion, compression, watermarking, signing, and more. Launched a knowledge base with 30+ practical guides — helping users understand their files and the tools available to them, completely free.

🎯 Our Mission

PDFSnap exists to prove that basic file tools don't need to cost money. The PDF format has been around for over 30 years. Modern browsers are powerful enough to handle PDF and image processing entirely on-device. There is no technical reason these tools should cost $20/month.

Our mission: give everyone — students, freelancers, small businesses, and professionals — access to the same tools that large companies pay for, completely free and with full privacy.

🔒 Privacy — How It Actually Works

When you use a PDFSnap tool, your file is loaded into your browser's memory using the JavaScript File API. The PDF.js and pdf-lib libraries then process it entirely on your device — no network request is ever made with your file data.

✅ Your files are never uploaded to any server
✅ No account required to use any tool
✅ No watermark added to your files
✅ No file size limits imposed by us
✅ Works offline after first page load
✅ We don't sell, share, or analyze your data

We use Google Analytics (anonymized) to understand which tools are most used, and Google AdSense to display ads that fund the site. Neither service receives your file content.

📢 How We Stay Free

PDFSnap is funded by non-intrusive display ads through Google AdSense. These ads allow us to cover hosting costs and keep developing new tools and guides — while keeping everything free for users.

We appreciate your support by whitelisting PDFSnap in your ad blocker. It directly helps us keep the tools free and improve the site.
