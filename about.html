🎯 Our Mission

Freepdfconverterr was created with one goal — to give everyone access to powerful PDF and image tools completely free of charge. We believe that basic file tools should not cost money or require you to upload your private files to unknown servers.

Everything on our platform runs entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server — they stay on your device at all times.
