1. Why Not Just Use Microsoft Edge?

Windows 11 ships with Microsoft Edge as the default PDF viewer, and it's actually decent for basic reading. It opens PDFs quickly, supports annotations like highlights and notes, and handles most standard files without issue. So why look for an alternative?

Edge falls short in several important areas that regular PDF users care about:

  • No form filling or saving — Edge lets you type into some PDF forms but often cannot save the filled fields properly.
  • No advanced search — Finding specific text across multiple pages is limited compared to dedicated readers.
  • No bookmarks panel — Long PDFs with internal navigation chapters are harder to manage.
  • Privacy concerns — Edge sends some browsing and document telemetry to Microsoft by default.
  • No text-to-speech or accessibility tools — For users who need read-aloud functionality, Edge's PDF viewer is basic.
For casual reading of a one-page PDF, Edge is perfectly fine. For anything involving filling forms, managing large documents, or handling lots of PDFs daily, a dedicated reader is much better.

4. Key Features to Look For in a Free PDF Reader

When choosing the best free PDF reader for Windows 11, these are the features that actually matter for day-to-day use:

Speed and Startup Time

A PDF reader you use many times a day needs to open fast. SumatraPDF opens in under a second. Adobe Reader can take 5–10 seconds on its first launch. If speed matters, choose accordingly.

Annotation Tools

Basic annotation — highlight text, add sticky notes, draw — is free in Foxit, PDF-XChange, and Adobe Reader. Make sure the annotations save correctly to the file so others can see them when you share.

Form Filling and Saving

Not all free readers can save filled PDF forms. Foxit and PDF-XChange do this reliably for free. Adobe Reader can fill and save standard AcroForms. SumatraPDF cannot fill forms at all.

Search Quality

Full-text search across a long document is essential for research. All five options in this list handle basic search. PDF-XChange has the most powerful search with regex support and case-sensitivity options.

Always test your chosen PDF reader by opening one of your most complex PDFs — a scanned contract, a form-filled document, or a large report. If it renders correctly and loads quickly, you've found your match.

6. How to Install and Set a Default PDF Reader on Windows 11

Once you've chosen your preferred free PDF reader, here's how to install it and make it open PDFs automatically by default:

1
Download your chosen readerGo to the official website of your chosen app (Foxit, SumatraPDF, etc.) and download the free installer. Always use the official site to avoid bundled software from third-party download sites.
2
Run the installerOpen the downloaded .exe file and follow the setup wizard. For SumatraPDF, the install takes under 30 seconds. For Adobe Reader, expect 5–10 minutes.
3
Set as default PDF viewerRight-click any PDF file on your computer → "Open with" → "Choose another app" → select your new reader → tick "Always use this app" → click OK. All PDFs will now open in your chosen reader automatically.
4
Alternative via SettingsGo to Windows Settings → Apps → Default apps → scroll down to ".pdf" → click it → choose your preferred app from the list.
Avoid downloading PDF readers from third-party sites like Softonic or CNET. These often bundle unwanted software with the installer. Always download directly from the developer's official website.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Adobe Acrobat Reader still free in 2026?
Yes, Adobe Acrobat Reader (the basic viewer) is still free. Adobe's paid product is Acrobat Pro, which adds editing, export, and advanced tools. The free Reader is sufficient for reading, annotating, and filling standard PDF forms.
What is the best lightweight PDF reader for Windows 11?
SumatraPDF is the lightest free PDF reader available — it's under 10MB and opens PDFs in under a second. It's perfect for older laptops or anyone who wants a fast, distraction-free reading experience. The trade-off is it's read-only with no annotation or form-filling.
Can I read PDFs offline with these readers?
Yes — all four desktop apps (Foxit, SumatraPDF, PDF-XChange, Adobe Reader) work completely offline once installed. PDFSnap requires an internet connection for initial load but processes your files locally in the browser.
Which free PDF reader lets me fill and save forms?
Foxit PDF Reader and Adobe Acrobat Reader both let you fill and save standard PDF forms for free. PDF-XChange also supports this. SumatraPDF does not support form filling at all — it's read-only.
Does Windows 11 have a built-in PDF reader?
Yes — Microsoft Edge is the default PDF viewer in Windows 11. It handles basic reading and simple highlighting, but lacks advanced features like reliable form saving, OCR, or tabbed viewing. Most users benefit from installing a dedicated reader.
Are these PDF readers safe to download?
All readers listed here are from reputable developers with millions of active users. Always download from the official developer website. SumatraPDF is fully open-source, making it the most transparently safe option. Foxit and PDF-XChange are established commercial companies with free tiers.
