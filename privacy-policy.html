🔒 Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

🎉 TL;DR — Your files NEVER leave your device. All processing happens in your browser. We cannot see your documents, period.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PDFSnap (https://pdfsnap.github.io/). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website.

2. No File Uploads — Ever

All file processing on PDFSnap happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do NOT upload, store, or access any files you process. Your files never leave your device — this is a technical certainty, not just a promise.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following non-personal information automatically via Google Analytics:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • General geographic location (country/city level only)

This is used only to understand which tools are popular and to improve our service. It is anonymized and aggregated.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display non-intrusive ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings.

5. Account Information

If you create an optional account, your name and email are stored locally in your browser only (localStorage). We do not store this on any server. Clearing your browser data removes it completely.

6. Bug Reports & Contact Forms

When you submit a bug report or contact form, the following is sent to Formspree (our form handler):

  • Tool name, issue type, and description you provide
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • Date of submission

No file data is ever included in these submissions.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Their own privacy policies apply:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage tracking
  • Google AdSense — non-intrusive display ads
  • Formspree — contact/bug report form handling

8. Your Rights

Since we don't store your personal data on any server, there is no data to delete or export. If you used our contact form, you can request deletion of that submission by emailing us directly.

9. Children's Privacy

PDFSnap does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of PDFSnap after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact
