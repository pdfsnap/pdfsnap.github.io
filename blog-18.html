Types of PDF Signatures Explained

Electronic Signature (e-Signature)

An image of your signature overlaid on the PDF. This is the most common type for everyday documents — employment contracts, rental agreements, NDAs, consent forms. It is legally valid in most countries for most document types. It looks like a real signature and is what you create when you draw your signature using your finger or mouse.

Digital Signature (Cryptographic)

A cryptographically verified signature embedded in the PDF using a digital certificate. This proves the document has not been modified since signing. Required for highly regulated industries — banking, government, healthcare in some jurisdictions. Much more complex to set up but provides the highest legal certainty.

Typed Signature

Your name typed in a signature-style font, placed on the document. Legally valid in many contexts, especially for online forms and low-stakes agreements.

📌 Which Type Do You Need?

For 95% of everyday documents — rental agreements, job offer letters, NDAs, freelance contracts — a simple electronic signature is perfectly valid. Only specialized financial, legal, and government documents require cryptographic digital certificates.

4 Free Ways to Sign a PDF in 2026

Method 1 — Adobe Acrobat Reader (Free Desktop)

Adobe Acrobat Reader (the free version) allows PDF signing. Open your PDF → click Fill and Sign in the right panel → click Sign → Draw, Type, or Upload your signature image → click where you want to place it → save. Available for Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android.

Method 2 — iPhone Markup (Built-in, Free)

Open the PDF in Files or Mail → tap the Markup icon → tap the Signature icon → draw your signature with your finger → tap Done → drag to position → save. Completely free, built into iOS.

Method 3 — Mac Preview (Built-in, Free)

Open the PDF in Preview → click Markup toolbar → click Signature icon → Create Signature using trackpad, camera, or iPhone → click your saved signature → drag to position. One of the best free PDF signing tools available anywhere.

Method 4 — Online Tools

DocuSign offers a free tier (3 documents per month). Smallpdf fill and sign works in browser with a free tier. Both upload your document to their servers — acceptable for non-sensitive documents.

✅ Best Method by Device

iPhone? Use iOS Markup. Mac? Use Preview. Windows? Use Adobe Acrobat Reader free. Android? Use Adobe Acrobat Reader app (free).

Is a Digital Signature Legally Valid?

Yes — in most countries, electronic signatures are legally binding for the vast majority of document types.

  • India: The Information Technology Act 2000 recognizes electronic signatures as legally valid.
  • USA: The E-SIGN Act makes electronic signatures legally binding for most commercial contracts.
  • EU: eIDAS regulation establishes legal validity across all EU member states.

Important exceptions: Electronic signatures are generally NOT valid for wills and testaments, real estate deeds in some jurisdictions, and certain government documents requiring certified digital certificates.

⚠️ Always Check With the Requesting Party

Before signing digitally, confirm the other party accepts electronic signatures. Most businesses do, but some traditional legal firms and government departments still require wet signatures on physical paper.

Pro Tips for Professional Signatures

  • Use a stylus on mobile for a much cleaner drawn signature than a finger.
  • Sign on white paper first, photograph it, then use that image as your signature. This gives the most authentic-looking digital signature.
  • Save your signature as PNG with transparent background so it looks clean on any document color.
  • Always add date alongside signature — most legal documents require a date next to the signature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sign a PDF on Android for free?
Yes — Adobe Acrobat Reader (free app) for Android has a built-in Fill and Sign feature. Tap the pencil icon, tap Sign, draw or type your signature, and place it on the document. Completely free.
Is typing my name as a signature legally valid?
In most cases yes. A typed name in a signature font is considered a valid electronic signature under most e-signature laws including India's IT Act. For high-value contracts, a drawn signature is more defensible if ever challenged.
How do I sign a PDF on just a phone?
On iPhone: open PDF in Files → tap Share → Markup → add signature. On Android: open in Adobe Acrobat Reader free → Fill and Sign → Sign. Both are completely free.
